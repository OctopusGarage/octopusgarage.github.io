# english-pilot

> English conversation gate, coaching assistant, and review system for AI workflows.

## Links

- GitHub: https://github.com/OctopusGarage/english-pilot

## Summary

english-pilot helps AI-assisted workflows stay English-dominant when needed. It provides a conversation gate, compact coaching, pronunciation support, and review history for people who want practical English feedback while working with agents.

## Best For

- English coaching inside agent workflows.
- Writing and phrasing review during technical work.
- Language-learning feedback that does not derail the main task.

## Keywords

English learning, conversation coach, writing feedback, language learning, MCP, TypeScript.
