# bracket-guess

> Predict knockout brackets and export polished shareable posters in the browser.

## Links

- GitHub: https://github.com/OctopusGarage/bracket-guess
- Demo: https://octopusgarage.github.io/bracket-guess/

## Summary

bracket-guess is a browser-based knockout bracket predictor. Users pick winners round by round and export a shareable poster. The app is built with React, TypeScript, Vite, and internationalization support.

## Best For

- World Cup and tournament bracket experiments.
- Pure frontend product demos.
- Shareable visual result exports without a backend.

## Keywords

Bracket prediction, World Cup, React, TypeScript, Vite, poster export, i18n.
